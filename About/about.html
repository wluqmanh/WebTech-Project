About Us

As food lovers, our team is passionate about making and consuming the best meals daily, whether it is home-made or at a restaurant. There are a lot of websites and blogs on cooking recipes in Malaysia, but most of them involve complex ingredients that may be hard to find supermarkets and the recipes are not classified as either halal or non-halal. Apart from that, it is difficult for Muslims to identify whether the recipe can be used or needs to be modified to suit their taste. Thus, we are doing a website that will overcome these problems by featuring quick and easy-to-make halal dishes for people of all ages. We also aim to promote the rich tastes of Malaysian cuisine to the public while providing simple recipes from different regions around the world. Some of the recipes include Japanese, Thai, and Western cuisines.
