French Toast

by Faisal Yasier


Description

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. A great choice for breakfast when you want something to fill your stomach but too lazy to cook.


Ingredients

Number of servings: 4

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 milk
  • 4 slices bread

Instruction

  1. To start making this easy French toast recipe you will need to beat an egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with Easy Spiced Syrup (recipe follows), if desired. Voila, easy French toast.
  4. 1/4 milk
  5. Easy Spiced Syrup:Easy Spiced Syrup: Add 1 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract and 1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Ground Cinnamon to 1 cup pancake syrup; stir well to mix. Serve warm, if desired.
