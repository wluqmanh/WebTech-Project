Fried Ice Cream

by Si Rashid


Description

During a hot and thirsty day we always want something to chill us. Eating ice cream might become an option here but can you imagine eating an ice cream that crispy at the outside while soft and cool inside? Stop being curious and lets make a fried ice cream!! The name is a bit crazy but wait until you take the first bite, you might ended blow your mind.


Ingredients

Number of servings: 1

  • 2 slice of bread
  • 1 scoop of ice cream

Instruction

  1. Take a slice of bread and press it center using a tablespoon.
  2. Put 1 scoop of ice cream at the center of the bread.
  3. Take another slice of bread and put on top of the ice cream.
  4. Use a plastic cup to take out the center of the bread with ice cream.
  5. Deep fry the bread with ice cream for few second and ready to serve.
