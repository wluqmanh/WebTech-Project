Sweet Potato Chips

by Aisyah Dems


Description

Want to have something to eat during study or working at home? Grab some sweet potatoes and lets make our own chips to fulfill mouth desire. To make these sweet potato chips extra crispy, slice the potatoes as thin as you can (a mandoline is very helpful here!) The chips will also crisp up as they cool so don't feel like you have to keep baking or they will get too dark.


Ingredients

Number of servings: 4

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground pepper
  • 2 green onion, finely sliced
  • 2/3 cup sour cream

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. Place two wire racks on two large rimmed baking sheets. Toss sweet potatoes in oil until evenly coated.
  2. In a separate large bowl, whisk together spices and cornstarch. Add sweet potatoes and toss until evenly coated in spice mixture.
  3. Place sweet potatoes on wire racks in a single layer. Bake for 25 to 35 minutes, until sweet potatoes look crispy and dry to touch. (They'll continue to crisp as they cool.)
  4. While the sweet potatoes cool, make dip: Fold green onions into sour cream and serve with sweet potato chips.
