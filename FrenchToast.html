French Toast

by Faisal Yasier

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. A great choice for breakfast when you want something to fill your stomach but too lazy to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 milk
  • 4 slices bread
